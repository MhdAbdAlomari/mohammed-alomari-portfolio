## What broke first

It wasn't performance — it was state. Specifically, edge cases in the order lifecycle that only appeared with real users doing unexpected things. Bloc helped us reproduce them; explicit state machines made them fixable.

## What saved us

Crashlytics, structured logging, and the discipline of treating every customer-reported issue as a missing test case.

## What I'd build in from day one

Feature flags. Even a simple boolean toggle, controlled remotely, would have saved a few stressful afternoons rolling things forward.

Stronger telemetry around the booking funnel — knowing where users drop off is what makes product decisions cheap.
